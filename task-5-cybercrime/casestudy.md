Case Study: The "QR Code" UPI Scam

* The Crime: UPI/Online Payment Fraud using fraudulent QR codes.
* How it Happens: The attacker contacts a student pretending to be a buyer for an item listed online or a representative offering a "cashback" reward. Instead of sending money, the attacker sends a QR code to the student via WhatsApp, claiming it must be scanned to "receive" the payment.
* The Trap: When the student scans the code and enters their UPI PIN, they are actually authorizing a debit from their own account rather than receiving a credit.
* Target: College students and young adults who frequently use UPI for quick transactions.
* Consequences: Immediate financial loss, potential compromise of banking linked mobile numbers, and emotional distress.
