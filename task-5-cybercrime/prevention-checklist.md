1. PIN is for Sending: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; it is only required for sending.
2. Verify the Source: Double-check the identity of anyone sending you a payment link or QR code.
3. Use Official Apps: Only use verified UPI apps from the Play Store/App Store.
4. Enable Biometrics: Set up fingerprint or face ID locks for your payment apps.
5. Check Transaction SMS: Always read the SMS from your bank carefully to see if money was "Debited" or "Credited".
6. Limit Public Wi-Fi: Avoid making financial transactions while connected to unsecured public Wi-Fi.
7. Set Daily Limits: Use your banking app to set a daily limit on UPI transactions to minimize potential loss.
8. Report Immediately: If scammed, call the National Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930 or visit cybercrime.gov.in immediately.
